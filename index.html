universAAL Open Platform

universAAL is a semantic and distributed software platform designed to ease the development of integrated Ambient Assisted Living applications.

The semantic nature of universAAL makes it ideal for highly heterogeneous environments, it’s power making it suitable for IoT (Internet of Things), wearables, Big Data, and many more domains.

universAAL is distributed as open source software with the Apache Software License 2.0

Objectives

  • Design and establish uStore. Inspired by Apple’s “App Store” concept, the uStore will provide a one-stop-shop for universAAL end-user services
  • Design and establish the universAAL developer depot.
  • Consolidate existing work and integrate with new development
  • Devise a technical strategy for achieving interoperability amongst universAAL platform elements
  • Design the platform to be open, and allow interoperability with existing systems / other domains.
  • Extend the range of actors involved in creating and operating AAL solutions.
  • Actively promote widespread adoption of the universAAL approach and platform.
  • Demonstrate the practical usefulness of the approach using a set of proof-of-concept scenarios
